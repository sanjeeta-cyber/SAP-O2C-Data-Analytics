# 📊 Order-to-Cash (O2C) Analysis with Data Analytics

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an end-to-end Order-to-Cash (O2C) business process integrated with data analytics. It demonstrates how sales data can be analyzed to generate meaningful business insights such as revenue trends, top customers, and product performance.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Understand the O2C lifecycle  
- Analyze sales data using Python  
- Generate insights for business decision-making  
- Visualize revenue trends and performance  

---

## 🏢 Business Scenario
Company: XYZ Retail Pvt. Ltd.  
Industry: Retail  

The company wants to analyze its sales performance, identify top customers, and track revenue trends to improve decision-making.

---

## 🔄 O2C Process Flow
Customer → Sales Order → Delivery → Invoice → Payment  

---

## 📊 Data Analytics
- Total Revenue Calculation  
- Revenue by Customer  
- Revenue by Product  
- Monthly Sales Trend  

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
- CSV Dataset

## 📈 Output
